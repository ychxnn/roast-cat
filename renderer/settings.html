Accessibility (macOS)

To read your AI CLI sessions and roast you based on your actual prompts, Roast Cat needs Accessibility permission.

System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility → enable Electron or Roast Cat.

Without this, the cat still roasts you on time of day and your real activity (focus time, breaks, late nights).

Reads your prompts from: Terminal & iTerm2 only (for now).
Health roasts (overwork, breaks, late nights) work everywhere — they use your activity, not your apps.
