Meet your Roast Cat

A tiny cat lives on your desktop and keeps an eye on your coding sessions. When you slip into a bad habit, it roasts you — with receipts, not vibes. Let's set yours up. Takes about 30 seconds.

✨

How it works

  • 🧠Catches bad habits. Context rot, error loops, panic-prompting, debugging with no context, unreviewed giant outputs.
  • 🫶Watches your health. Knows when you're actually working — and roasts you for no breaks, long days, 2am sessions.
  • 😼It has moods. It loafs, plays, naps, side-eyes you — and roasts in a style that matches the mood.
  • 🖱️Click it for an instant roast, drag it anywhere.

One last thing
