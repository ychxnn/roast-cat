Accessibility (macOS)

To read your AI CLI sessions and roast you based on your actual prompts, Roast Cat needs Accessibility permission.

System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility → enable Electron or Roast Cat.

Without this, the cat still roasts you based on time of day, health facts, and which AI app is in focus.

Monitored apps (when permission granted):
Terminal · iTerm2 · Warp — Claude CLI, Aider, Codex CLI, llm, sgpt
Chrome · Arc · Safari · Brave — claude.ai, chatgpt.com, grok.x.com, gemini.google.com, perplexity.ai
